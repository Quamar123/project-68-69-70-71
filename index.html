In other words, the labs and library are the logical extension of classroom learning. Computer lab

Today in this digital era, Information Technology has become an integral part of our living style. Bhashyam has reacted to the change positively and has established a modern technology center with latest hardware and software in its residential campuses. With an ideal student-machine ratio, experienced faculty will be offering computer education to students from VI standard. It is in the process of establishing technology center in other campuses to impart information technology training.

Activities

Sports

Bhashyam believes in the dictum 'A healthy mind in a healthy body'. Students are provided with excellent facilities for games like cricket, badminton, volleyball, basketball and so on. Bhashyam has first to its credit to start Action Cricket in the region. Students are encouraged to participate in inter-school competitions held within the group schools. Eminent sports personalities are invited to motivate students during the major events in the school. Outstanding sportsmen who show promise of becoming a reputed name in the field of sports are adopted by Bhashyam sports academy. These students are recognized, rewarded and awarded during the annual day celebrations.

Extra curricular activities

To hone the creative talents in the students, Bhashyam has formed hobby clubs, where students can join several leisure activities like arts and crafts, music, dance, rangoli, debates, literary activities, gardening and so on. Each club has an identity, style and agenda of its own. Students could choose to be a member of any one or a group of clubs, pursue activities of interest and hone their skills. These activities under the guidance of the experienced people provide a proper channel for creative thinking and also act as safety release for bottled up emotions.

whats new

  • IIT Preparation

    The pattern of education in the higher sections of Bhashyam IIT Academy has undergone a paradigm shift and goes beyond the stipulated syllabus. It provides a stress free education while preparing the students for future Engineering and Medical competitive examinations. For IIT Foundation students, Bhashyam has designed interactive and practical based learning that includes group discussions, seminars and workshops. Several projects are allotted to students in groups and they are asked to make their presentations in the classroom after completing their project. This will also give Bhashyam students a head start at the intermediate level to face the competitive exams like IIT-JEE, AIEEE, AFMC, AIIMS and JIPMER confidently and be assured of success.

  • campus blooms

    The Campus Blooms. All the Bhashyam residential campuses are located in serene locations to help students pursue education with undivided attention. Elaborate security arrangements have been made in the residential premises to ensure safety of the child at all times. Bhashyam always believes that nothing can substitute parental affection and care. The family bonding cannot be replaced. It can however be replicated through affection and love. Efforts have been made to retain the nuances of homely atmosphere. The campuses are provided with excellent infrastructure with adequate landscapes. As the academic block is close to the residential wing, the students get ample time to spend with their teaching faculty. This not only helps them to stay tuned with their academics but also strengthen their bond with teaching faculty.
